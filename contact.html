About Us

Monad University is a prestigious institution committed to fostering intellectual growth, innovation, and a diverse learning environment.Established by UP State Govt. Act 23 of 2010 & U/S 2(f) of U.G.C. Act 1956, we are dedicated to shaping future leaders who can make an impact in their respective fields and contribute to global progress. Located in the heart of Pilkhuwa, our campus offers state-of-the-art facilities, a dynamic community of scholars, and a rich cultural heritage that blends tradition with modernity. We offer a wide range of undergraduate, graduate, and professional programs designed to challenge and inspire students to excel academically and personally.
